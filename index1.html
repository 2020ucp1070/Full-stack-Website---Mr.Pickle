Our Story

Jhansi Pacchallu was started by a passionate housewife dedicated to making authentic Andhra pickles. With a tradition of over 33 years, our pickles are crafted to make each bite a delightful experience. It's more than just a business—it's a tradition that brings the true taste of Andhra to every table.We feel that everyone should taste our pickles at least once to tell this is true. We are backed by a couple of grannies, who fed few hundred families in their decades of homemade pickling experience.All we can say is, Taste it once and you are sure to come back for more – Our pickles are a feast to taste buds

Our pickles are made from 100% home-made recipes with fresh, high-quality ingredients sourced from nature. Each pickle is prepared as if it were for our own family, using the finest oils to enhance the flavors.We were doing well with our homemade mommy-granny pickles and our customers asked “why don’t you try to share and supply us our childhood memories?” We were shocked and then our customer said, by tasting our pickles, he got to remember the taste of his granny pickles from childhood and so the nostalgic memories turned back in his life

Encouraged by our customers who reminisced about their childhood memories of pickles made by their grannies, we decided to share this tradition with everyone. We also offer exotic and rare fruits and flowers, such as Thati Munjalu, Thati Tegalu, Regi Pallu, Eetha Pallu, Kale Kayalu, and Bikki Kayalu, which are reminiscent of the street vendors' delights from the past.These so called exotic varieties were street fruits & flowers which vendors used to sell in our days. We started offering products like Thati Munjalu, Thati Tegalu, Regi Pallu, Eetha Pallu, Kale Kayalu, Bikki Kayalu and many more
